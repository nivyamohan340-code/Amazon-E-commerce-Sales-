# Amazon E-commerce Sales and Fulfillment Performance Analysis
# Domain:
E-commerce Sales and Supply Chain Analytics
# Objective:
To analyze Amazon E-commerce sales data in order to understand Sales performance,Product demand,fulfillment efficiency, and shipping patterns,and to identify factors that influence revenue and order success.
# Business Problem: 
This project aims to analyze Amazon e-commerce sales data to identify sales trends, high-demand products, regional sales distribution, and fulfillment performance, helping improve business decision-making and operational efficiency.
# Dataset:
Amazon sales data set containing 128975 rows and 23 features.It contains detailed information about customer orders, product details, sales values, shipping locations, and fulfillment status. 
The Messy and Cleaned dataset used in this project is stored in Google Drive due to size limitations

# Top 5 Key Insights
Sales Are Highly Concentrated in a Few States
South Region Is the Strongest Revenue Contributor
Category Demand Varies Significantly by Region
High-Value Orders Appear as Sales Outliers
Underperforming Regions Present Growth Opportunities
# Business Recommendations
Focus on top-performing states to secure stable revenue.
Adopt region-specific product and marketing strategies.
Increase average order value through bundling and premium offers.
Convert one-time high-value buyers into repeat customers.
Use insights from data visualization to guide strategic decisions.

# Final Conclusion
This analysis reveals that sales performance is driven primarily by regional demand patterns and product preferences, rather than uniform customer behavior. By focusing on high-performing regions, tailoring category strategies, and addressing underperforming markets, the business can significantly improve overall sales and long-term growth.
